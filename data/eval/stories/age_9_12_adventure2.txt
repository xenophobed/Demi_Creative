The rules of the Midnight Library were simple: you could borrow any book, but you had to return it before sunrise or the story would forget you were reading it — and rewrite itself without you. Jin had borrowed "The Cartographer's Secret" three times, and each time the ending changed. The first time, the cartographer found the lost city. The second time, the city found the cartographer. The third time, Jin opened to the last page and found a hand-drawn map with her own street on it, and a note in unfamiliar handwriting: "Your turn to explore."
