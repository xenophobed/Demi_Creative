Captain Zara and her robot parrot, Bolt, sailed their sky-ship through the Cotton Candy Clouds. "Storm ahead!" squawked Bolt. A massive rainbow tornado swirled before them. "We fly through it!" Zara shouted, gripping the wheel. The ship spun, colors flashing everywhere. When they burst out the other side, they found themselves above a floating island covered in crystals. "Best shortcut ever," Zara grinned. Bolt just shook his metal feathers and beeped disapprovingly.
