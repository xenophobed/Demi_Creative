Marco found a map tucked inside an old library book. It showed a path through the school basement to a hidden room. After school, he followed the map past dusty shelves and forgotten boxes. Behind a rusty door, he found a room full of inventions — a helmet that translated animal sounds, boots that could walk on ceilings, and a pen that drew things into existence. A note on the wall read: "For the curious. Use wisely." Marco picked up the pen and drew a door back to the library.
