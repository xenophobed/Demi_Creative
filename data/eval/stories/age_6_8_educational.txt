Did you know that octopuses have three hearts and blue blood? Two hearts pump blood to the gills, and one pumps it to the rest of the body. When an octopus swims fast, the heart that delivers blood to the body actually stops beating. That is why octopuses prefer to crawl rather than swim — swimming exhausts them! They are also incredibly smart. Some octopuses have been seen unscrewing jar lids from the inside to escape.
