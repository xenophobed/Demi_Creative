In a cozy village at the edge of a great forest, a girl named Lily discovered that her old music box could play songs she had never heard before. Each night, a new melody would drift through her room, and tiny fireflies would gather by her window to listen. One evening, the music box played a lullaby so beautiful that even the wind stopped to hear it. The fireflies spelled out the words "sweet dreams" in golden light, and Lily smiled as she drifted off to the most wonderful sleep.
