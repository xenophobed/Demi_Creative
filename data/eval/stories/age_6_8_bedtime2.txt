Every night, the old lighthouse keeper climbed one hundred and twelve steps to light the lamp. But tonight was different. When he reached the top, a small dragon was curled around the lamp, glowing softly. "I got lost," the dragon said quietly. "The stars all look the same from up here." The keeper smiled and pointed south. "Follow the three bright ones in a row — that is Orion's Belt. It will guide you home." The dragon stretched its tiny wings and flew into the night, leaving a trail of warm golden light.
